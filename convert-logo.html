Alternative Methods to Convert PDF to PNG:

Method 1 - Online Tools:

  • Visit pdf2png.com
  • Upload your PDF file at /Users/tylerdiorio/avidsignswrapswebsite/public/logo.pdf
  • Download the converted PNG

Method 2 - macOS Preview:

  • Open the PDF in Preview app
  • Go to File → Export As...
  • Choose PNG format
  • Set resolution to 300 DPI for high quality

Method 3 - Command Line (if you have ImageMagick):

convert -density 300 logo.pdf -resize 200x -quality 100 logo.png
